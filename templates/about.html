What is Bittensor?

Bittensor is a decentralized network that incentivizes the development and deployment of AI models. It creates a marketplace where AI developers can monetize their models while users can access a diverse range of AI services. The network is powered by the TAO token, which serves as both a reward mechanism and governance token, playing a role in governance through staking and subnet funding decisions.

Unlike traditional AI services that are controlled by a few large corporations, Bittensor distributes AI capabilities across a global network of participants, making AI more accessible, transparent, and resistant to censorship.
